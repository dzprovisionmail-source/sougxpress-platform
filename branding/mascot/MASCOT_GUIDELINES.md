# Mascot Guidelines

## Role of the Mascot
The SougXpress mascot is the official face of the platform, personifying the brand's friendliness and efficiency.

## Usage
The mascot will appear in:
- App Splash Screens
- Onboarding Flows
- Marketing Materials
- Social Media
- Delivery Vehicles & Packaging

## Official Versions
Only mascot designs in `mascot/approved/` are official. Concepts in `mascot/concepts/` remain editable and non-official.
